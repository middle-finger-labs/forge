# Forge — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 25, 2026**

Middle Finger Labs ("we", "us", "our") operates the Forge mobile application (the "App"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Display name (optional)

### Device Information
- Push notification tokens (APNs for iOS, FCM for Android)
- Device type and OS version (for compatibility)
- Biometric authentication status (enrolled/not enrolled — we never access biometric data itself)

### Usage Data
- Pipeline creation and approval actions
- Agent conversation messages (stored on your server, not ours)
- App usage analytics (crash reports, feature usage — anonymized)

## Information We Do NOT Collect
- Biometric data (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint data never leaves your device)
- Source code or repository contents
- Keychain or secure storage contents
- Location data
- Contact lists or phone data

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the App
- To send push notifications you've opted into
- To authenticate your identity
- To improve app stability and performance

## Data Storage
- All conversation data and pipeline information is stored on **your self-hosted Forge server**
- Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
- Push notification tokens are stored on your Forge server for notification routing
- No conversation or pipeline data is stored on our servers

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized crash reports with our error tracking provider to improve app stability.

## Security
- All network communication uses TLS/SSL encryption
- Authentication tokens are stored in platform-secure storage
- Biometric authentication is handled entirely by the OS security framework
- WebSocket connections are encrypted

## Your Rights
You can:
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Disable push notifications in your device settings
- Remove biometric enrollment from the App settings
- Request a copy of your data by contacting us

## Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
